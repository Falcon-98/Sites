How this works

Lossless mode: Removes metadata, thumbnails, duplicate objects, and unused resources. Text, fonts, and vector graphics stay 100% unchanged. Typically 15–35% reduction.

Balanced mode: Does everything above, plus intelligently recompresses JPEG images. Reduces quality from, e.g., 95% to 85% in a way that's imperceptible to the human eye. Can achieve 30–60% reduction on image-heavy files.

Unlike rasterization tools, this approach keeps your PDF fully searchable, copyable, and preserves all original content fidelity.
