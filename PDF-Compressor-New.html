How this works

Photoshop PDFs are typically large because they contain full-resolution rasterized images. This tool:

  • Analyzes your PDF to detect page complexity
  • Renders each page to an image at the quality level you choose
  • Re-compresses as high-quality JPEG and rebuilds the PDF
  • Uses intelligent compression to preserve visual quality while cutting size

Trade-off: Pages become rasterized images (no text selection), but look nearly identical at your chosen quality level.

Everything runs in your browser. Your file never leaves your computer.
