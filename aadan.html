Why Join

Alumni Benefits

Membership in AADAN connects you to a rich ecosystem of professional support, community, and lifelong growth.

🚀

Career Development

  • →Job placement support and referrals within the nutrition sector
  • →Professional mentoring from experienced alumni
  • →Invitations to career fairs, industry events, and workshops
  • →Access to a dedicated AADAN job board and vacancy notifications
  • →Resume and interview coaching sessions
🎓

Assistance in Further Studies

  • →Guidance on postgraduate opportunities locally and abroad
  • →Scholarship and bursary information sharing among members
  • →Research collaboration and co-authorship opportunities
  • →Support from senior alumni who have pursued advanced degrees
🏛️

Access Facilities in the Department

  • →Use of departmental laboratories for professional development
  • →Library access and e-resource subscriptions
  • →Seminar rooms for alumni-organised events
  • →Priority booking of departmental facilities
📄

Copy of Documents & References

  • →Assistance in obtaining academic transcripts and certificates
  • →Reference letters supported by the department and AADAN
  • →Certified copies of relevant departmental documents
  • →Quick turnaround facilitation for official communications
📚

Support Lifelong Learning

  • →Continuous professional development workshops and webinars
  • →Access to a curated digital learning resource library
  • →Annual nutrition symposia and guest lecture series
  • →Discounted registration for CPD-accredited programmes
🌐

Network with the DAN Community

  • →Exclusive alumni directory connecting graduates across generations
  • →Annual reunions, felicitations, and social gatherings
  • →Private alumni social media and communication groups
  • →Collaborations with nutrition professionals from industry and academia
Give Back

Support Current Students

Alumni and donors play a vital role in shaping the next generation of nutrition professionals. Your contribution — big or small — creates a lasting impact.

💻

Computer & Technology Donations

Donate computers, tablets, and IT equipment to help students access digital learning resources and conduct modern nutritional research.

💰

Financial Sponsorships

Sponsor deserving students through bursaries, scholarship top-ups, or direct financial assistance to cover tuition, accommodation, or study materials.

🏥

Medical & Health Facilities

Contribute to improving on-campus medical facilities, first-aid resources, and student wellness programmes within the faculty.

🔬

Laboratory & Research Equipment

Fund the procurement of scientific equipment that enhances the quality of nutrition research and practical training in the department.

📖

Library & Study Resources

Donate textbooks, journal subscriptions, and e-learning licences to keep the departmental library current and accessible to all students.

🌱

Mentorship & Internship Placement

Offer your expertise by mentoring students or providing internship opportunities in your organisation, bridging academia with industry practice.

Membership

How to Join AADAN

Becoming a member is simple. Follow the steps below to join the AADAN community and start enjoying all the benefits of membership.

1

Download Form

Download the official Membership Application Form from the AADAN website or collect it from the Department of Applied Nutrition.

2

Read Constitution

Review the AADAN Constitution to understand the rights, responsibilities, and code of conduct expected of all members.

3

Make Payment

Pay the membership fee via bank transfer and obtain a bank slip as proof of payment. Details will be provided with the application form.

4

Submit Application

Email your completed form along with the scanned bank slip to aluminidan@wyab.ac.lk
